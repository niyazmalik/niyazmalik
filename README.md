# Niyaz Malik - A failure, learning since **April 9, 2025**

---

God is one for Muslims, Christians, Hindus, Buddhists, and many more. He sent all the scriptures: the ones before the Vedas, then the Vedas, the torah, the gospel, and the last of them, the Qur’an. He sent men as messangers to warn people and to guide them, and the last of them was a man named Muhammad.

We are the people of knowledge, and no people before us had this level of access to true knowledge, yet in this era of knowledge, almost all are still ignorant and selfish. We have information at our fingertips, yet many do not reason. And those who do gain knowledge often pursue it only to earn money, instead of using it to praise their Lord and to become beings who can control thier desires.

People save money for their future instead of helping the poor who need it in the present. People build huge houses in this temporary world. The rich keep getting richer, and the poor keep getting poorer. Some people simp for others, some blindly follow others, some worship things they themselves create, some worship the living and some worship the dead.

And some busy killing others in the name of God.

God is one, without any second, the Creator of the universe that we see and of all that lies beyond our sight. He is the One who gave us the gift of knowledge and made death the only certain truth and to him is our final return.
